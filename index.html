What makes this different

Survival stays vanilla

No creative, no /fly, death still hurts. Minecraft 1.21.x underneath. The economy is layered on top without changing the core game.

Real stakes

Fisher Pounds are earned by playing and spent on land, goods, and services. Miss rent three days in a row and you lose your plot. Prices are set by players.

Proper events

Dragon resets, Wither hunts, world border expansions, and seasonal Jobs multiplier weeks. There is always something coming up on the calendar.

Your first day

  1. Spawn in Saffron. Read the notice board near spawn for current rules and open bounties.
  2. Pick two jobs with /jobs browse. Start earning immediately.
  3. Rent a plot with /plot claim. Costs £5/day. This is your home base.
  4. Visit the market district. Player chest shops sell tools and materials.
  5. Check #bounty-board in Discord. Other players post paid contracts for resources.
  6. Check the Calendar to see what events are coming up.

How the economy works

  • Earning: Jobs Reborn pays per action (ore mined, mob killed, crop harvested). You can also run chest shops, fill player contracts on the bounty board, collect stock dividends, or lend money at interest.
  • Spending: Plot rent is due every Sunday. Buy goods from other players. Fast travel costs money. Cosmetics are available as money sinks.
  • Property: Towny manages land. Buy a plot in an existing town or found your own. Mayors set rent and rules.
  • Shops: Place a chest, right-click it, and set a price. It sells to players while you are offline.
  • Stocks: Once your shop earns enough, go public and issue shares. Shareholders earn weekly dividends.
  • Banks: Deposit to earn 0.5%/week interest. Take loans at 2%/week. Miss repayments and your balance is frozen.

FAQ

Do I need to pay real money?

No. Fisher Pounds are an in-game currency only. Nothing costs real money. Donations are cosmetics-only and never required.

What if I cannot play much? Will I lose my stuff?

Your inventory is safe. The only thing that needs attention is plot rent (£5/day). If you are going away, tell Seb and it can be paused. The bounty board lets you earn passively if others buy from your chest shop.

I do not like PvP. Is it forced?

No. PvP is off inside all towns. It is only on in the wilderness and in dedicated arenas like the Wither Hunt arena. You can play the whole economy without fighting anyone.

How do I earn money without mining?

Run a chest shop and sell to miners. Fill contracts on the bounty board. Buy shares in profitable shops and collect dividends. Lend your balance to other players at interest. Pick the Farmer or Hunter jobs.

Where do I see what is happening?

The Market page shows current prices. Stocks shows share prices. The Ledger shows recent payouts. The Calendar shows upcoming events. Discord has live chat and announcements.
