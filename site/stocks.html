Stock Exchange

Index stocks track materials on a slow random walk. Player IPOs are shares in actual player-owned shops, paying weekly dividends from shop revenue.

SymbolNamePrice24h

How to IPO your shop

  1. Your QuickShop must have at least £5,000 in weekly revenue.
  2. Run /ipo <ticker> <shares> <price> in-game.
  3. 10% of weekly shop revenue is paid to shareholders as dividend.
  4. You keep majority control as long as you hold >50% of shares.
