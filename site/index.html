What makes this different

Survival stays vanilla

No creative, no /fly, death still hurts. The game underneath is just Minecraft 1.21.x. Everything else is layered on top without changing the core experience.

A real economy

Fisher Pounds (£) are earned by working and spent on land, goods, and services. Rent is due every Sunday. Prices are set by players. Miss rent three times and you lose your plot.

Earn outside the game

Link your account here to earn £ from this site. Daily check-ins, mini-games, verified chores, and real work hours all pay out. Good if you want to stay in the economy without grinding Minecraft every day.

Your first day

  1. Spawn in Saffron. Check the notice board near spawn for current laws and open bounties.
  2. Pick two jobs with /jobs browse. Start earning immediately from mining, farming, hunting, or building.
  3. Rent a plot in town with /plot claim. Costs £5/day. This is your home base.
  4. Visit the market district. Player chest shops sell tools and materials.
  5. Check #bounty-board in Discord. Other players post paid contracts for resources they need.
  6. Link your account below and claim your first daily check-in on the Earn page for a free £3.

How the economy works

  • Earning: Jobs Reborn pays per action (ore mined, mob killed, crop harvested). You can also run chest shops, fill player contracts, collect stock dividends, or earn from this site.
  • Spending: Plot rent is due every Sunday. Buy goods from other players. Fast travel costs money. Cosmetics are available as money sinks.
  • Property: Towny manages land. Buy a plot in an existing town or found your own. Mayors set rent and rules.
  • Shops: Place a chest, right-click it, and set a price. It sells to players while you are offline.
  • Stocks: Once your shop earns enough, go public and issue shares. Other players buy in and earn weekly dividends.
  • Banks: Deposit to earn 0.5% weekly interest. Take loans at 2% weekly. Miss repayments and your balance is frozen.

FAQ

Do I need to pay real money for anything?

No. Fisher Pounds (£) are an in-game currency only. Nothing costs real money. Donations are cosmetics-only and never required.

What if I cannot play much? Will I lose my stuff?

Your inventory is safe. The only thing that needs attention is plot rent (£5/day). If you are going away, tell Seb and it can be paused. You can also earn rent money from this site without logging into Minecraft.

I do not like PvP. Is it forced?

No. PvP is disabled inside all towns. It is only on in the wilderness and in dedicated arenas. You can play the whole economy without ever fighting another player.

What is this website for?

It is a companion site that connects to your Minecraft account. Earn small amounts of in-game money by checking in daily, playing browser mini-games, logging real-world tasks (chores, gym sessions), or logging work hours. Especially useful if you want to stay involved without playing Minecraft every day.

How do I earn money without mining?

Run a chest shop and sell to miners. Fill contracts on the bounty board. Buy shares in profitable shops and collect dividends. Lend money to other players at interest. Or use this site to earn from chores and daily check-ins.

Where do I see what is happening on the server?

The Market page shows current item prices. The Stocks page shows share prices. The Ledger shows recent payouts. The Calendar shows upcoming events. The Discord server has live chat and announcements.
