Market Prices

Current buy and sell prices from player chest shops across the server. Prices are set by players, not admins. If something is cheap, someone is underselling. If it is expensive, there is a shortage.

Bid = what players are paying. Ask = what players are selling for. Spread = the gap between them. Once the bridge is live, this will update every 60 seconds from the server.

How to set up a shop

  1. Place a chest anywhere in your plot.
  2. Put the item you want to sell inside it.
  3. Right-click the chest and follow the QuickShop prompts to set a price.
  4. Players can buy from it while you are offline. Coins go straight to your balance.

You can also create a buying shop to purchase items from other players automatically.
