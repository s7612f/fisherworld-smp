How to earn in-game

The site pays pocket change. The real money is made in Minecraft.

  • Jobs Reborn: mine, farm, hunt, fish, or build. Pick two jobs with /jobs browse.
  • Chest shops: place a chest, right-click it, set a price. Sell to other players while offline.
  • Bounty board: take open contracts in #bounty-board on Discord. Deliver the goods, get paid.
  • Stock dividends: hold shares in profitable shops and collect weekly payouts.
  • Player loans: lend your balance to other players via the bank and charge interest.
