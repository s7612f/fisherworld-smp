How to earn in-game

  • Jobs Reborn — mine, farm, hunt, fish, build. Pick two jobs at a time.
  • Chest shops — sell goods to other players in your own shop.
  • Bounty board — take open contracts from other players in #bounty-board.
  • Stock dividends — hold shares in profitable shops, collect weekly payouts.
  • Player loans — lend your balance to other players at interest via the bank.
